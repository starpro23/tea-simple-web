About Us

At Tea Haven, we believe in the magic of tea. Our journey began with a simple passion for crafting the perfect cup, and today, we are proud to share our love for tea with you. From the lush tea gardens of Kenya to your cup, we bring you a selection of the finest teas, each with its own unique story and flavor.

Services

Tea Services

Explore a diverse menu of world-class teas, each carefully selected and brewed to highlight its unique flavor profile and soothing aroma. From bold blacks to calming herbals, there's something for every palate.

Take-Out

Take your favorite tea with you wherever you go. Our take-out service ensures your drink stays fresh and perfectly packaged, giving you comfort and energy on your way to work or during a busy day.

Catering

Make your occasions memorable with our tailored tea catering. Whether it's a formal event or casual gathering, we provide quality tea service along with handcrafted tea treats and warm hospitality.

Gift Baskets

Share the love of tea through our beautifully arranged gift baskets. They include premium teas, sweet accompaniments, and elegant tea tools, ideal for birthdays, holidays, or simply saying thank you.
